Answers to Exercise 5

h2

This selector makes the contain of h2 tag has blue background color and opacity of 0.5. It means the text inside h2 is faded.

div

This selector makes the contain of div tag has absolute postion and top has 100px and left has 200 px.

.pos

This selector makes the contain of .pos tag has absolute position and top has 150px.

table,th,td

This selector makes the contain of table,th,td tag has the border that is 1px with solid violet color.

td.bg

This selector makes the contain of td.bg tag has a green background color .

td.bd

This selector makes the contain of td.bd tag has a dashed border style.

th.padding

This selector makes the contain of th.padding tag the top padding is a 20px and bottom padding is 20px this means that the header cell of the table has space of 20 px top and bottom around the table.

.user1

This selector means that the text contained in user1 is aligned to the right with a violet color.

#user2

This selector means that the text contained in user2 is aligned to the center with a italic font-style.
